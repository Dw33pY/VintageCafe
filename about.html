Our Story

Over 130 years of warmth, flavor, and community.

Since 1892

A Place Where Time Stands Still

Vintage Cafe has been a cornerstone of the Everett community since 1892. What started as a small kitchen serving hearty meals to dockworkers and loggers has grown into one of the Pacific Northwest's most beloved gathering spots.

Through world wars, depressions, booms, and transformations, our doors have never closed on the promise of a warm meal and a welcoming smile. Today, we honor that legacy with every plate we serve.

Through the Years

Our Heritage

1892

Our doors first open on Hewitt Avenue, serving breakfast and lunch to the workers building a young city called Everett.

1920s

Prohibition couldn't slow us down. We became known for our coffee and pies — a refuge during uncertain times.

1950s

The post-war boom brings new flavors to our menu. Classic American breakfasts become our signature, alongside global dishes from returning service members.

1980s

A new generation takes the helm, refreshing the space while preserving the soul. Vintage French Toast becomes a legend.

Today

With 1,723 reviews and a 4.4-star rating, we're still the place where Everett gathers — for breakfast, lunch, and everything in between.
